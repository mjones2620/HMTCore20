About Me

Welcome! It takes a great amount of courage to make the first step to living your best life. Positive healthy mind, positive healthy body. What’s your mantra? I know how it feels to be so anxious and unmotivated you feel life will never improve. I am not only a Licensed Professional Counselor with over seven years experience providing direct patient care to adults, adolescents, children, and families, but I am your average every day girl who has dealt with her own array of challenges. By changing my thoughts and developing positive, healthy coping skills, I created a positive healthy mind, body and soul. I provide a safe, supportive, encouraging environment by creating a solid foundation for personal healing and development. I work to create a relationship with you to promote success and empowerment. I recognize you are the expert on your life and take a collaborative approach to achieve identified goals. I use a strength-based approach, building on your positive qualities. I recognize everyone measures success differently and tailor treatment to best meet your needs. Are you struggling with every day anxieties that are starting to impact your relationships, job, or home life? Do you feel so depressed you have lost motivation to do things you used to enjoy? Do you have a teenager who seems distant but has hinted they would like someone to talk to? If you can relate to any of this, then you’ve come to the right place. Healthy Minds Therapy will help you become the person you want to be!

I received my Bachelor of Science in Psychology from Virginia Tech in 2006. I earned my Master of Arts in Community Counseling from American Psychological Association (APA) accredited Argosy University School of Professional Psychology Northern Virginia in 2009. I completed my Post Master’s Certification in School Counseling at University of South Florida in 2014. During my journey as a therapist thus far, I have had the pleasure of working with clients in a variety of settings to include in-home, psychiatric hospitals, mental health clinics, and outpatient/private practice settings.
